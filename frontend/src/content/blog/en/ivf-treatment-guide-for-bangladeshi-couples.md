Fertility treatment can feel overwhelming, especially when it involves crossing a border. Over the past few years, India has become a leading destination for Bangladeshi couples seeking IVF, thanks to experienced specialists, modern labs and significant cost savings.

## Why consider IVF in India?

- Lower treatment costs without compromising success rates
- English- and Bengali-speaking coordinators
- Short flight times from Dhaka, Chittagong or Sylhet
- Transparent pricing for medication, injections and monitoring

## What to prepare before you travel

1. Recent semen analysis and ovarian reserve tests
2. Medical history summary from your local gynaecologist
3. Passport and visa documents for both partners
4. A rough travel window of 2–3 weeks for the initial cycle

## Choosing the right clinic

Not every fertility centre is equally experienced with international patients. We recommend clinics that have a dedicated international desk, in-house embryology lab, published success rates and clear communication in your preferred language.

## Costs to expect

A complete IVF cycle in India typically starts around $3,500–$5,000, including stimulation medication, egg retrieval, embryo transfer and early monitoring. Frozen embryo transfers and advanced techniques like ICSI or PGT add to the cost.
