Patient Story: Mohammed's Hip Replacement Journey from the UAE to India

By Khan Meditour Team July 5, 2026 Updated on July 14, 2026

Mohammed, a 64-year-old retiree living in Dubai, had been managing hip pain for years. Local quotes for total hip replacement were far beyond his budget, and waiting times were long. After a friend recommended Khan Meditour, he decided to explore treatment in India.

Step 1: Reports and opinion

Mohammed sent his X-rays and medical summary via WhatsApp. Within 48 hours, our medical team connected him with a senior orthopedic surgeon in Delhi who confirmed he was a good candidate for a ceramic total hip replacement.

Step 2: Fixed-cost estimate and travel plan

We provided a single all-inclusive estimate covering surgery, implant, hospital stay, physiotherapy, accommodation and airport transfers. No hidden charges, no surprises.

Step 3: Surgery and recovery

Mohammed and his wife flew into Delhi. A coordinator met them at the airport, helped with hospital admission and stayed in touch daily. The surgery went smoothly, and he began walking with support the next day.

Step 4: Follow-up back home

After two weeks in India, Mohammed returned to Dubai. We arranged video follow-ups with the surgeon and a local physiotherapy plan. Six months later, he is walking pain-free and has recommended Khan Meditour to two friends.

“I was nervous about travelling for surgery, but every detail was handled. The team treated me like family.” — Mohammed A.
